What are some of the ways to increase your neuroplasticity?

There are a couple ways to increase your neuroplasticity, the first one being to play video games.
The benefits associated with gaming include improvements in memory, reaction time, motor coordination, and cooperation.
Another one is by learning a new language. In one 2012 study, researchers examined 10 English-native exchange students who were studying German in Switzerland.
Their proficiency in German had improved after five months of intensive language training, and their gray matter density had as well.
This went on to improve one’s attention span, memory, and motor skills. So in short, just play lots of video games and learn some languages.
