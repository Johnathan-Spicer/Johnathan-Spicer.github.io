A time you were stuck on a simple problem

I remember during the fourth sprint I was stuck on the Super FizzBuzz challenge.
After looking up any sort of method that could aid in my task, and after re-reading the problem multiple times and trying out different ways to complete it, I decided to watch Joseph’s live stream for some help.
Sure enough, he gave me all the help I needed to complete my work. I learned that instead of trying my hardest to fix something on my own, I should’ve reached out to my facilitators or my group for help.
I would’ve completed the work faster, and more time to focus on other parts of the tests.
